About me

When I am not working, I am watching series or movies, listening to podcasts, tweeting, traveling, or (very occasionally) cooking. I am an advocate of aerobic exercise and low-carbohydrate dieting (although I don't always do a great job of adhering). I am a jazz drum enthusiast and enjoy following football and watching games with my friends for fun. My favorite singer is Leslie Cheung and my favorite team is Imagine Dragons.
